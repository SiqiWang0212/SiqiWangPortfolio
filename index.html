Research Replication
in STATA

This project is a replication exercise that replicates Angrist-Evans'(1998)'s findings
on the effect of having an extra child on female labor force participation and earnings.
This project obtained USA 1980 Census's raw data from 'Ipums International',
cleaned and shaped data, and used instrumental variable strategy for regressions.

Domestic Violence
during Covid-19
Tableau Visualization

This project provides insights into how ENDGBV addressed challenges in combating domestic violence during the pandemic in NYC. It targets individuals interested in public service and its impact, helping readers understand how unexpected events like pandemics can worsen domestic and gender gaps and highlight the importance of timely remote measures for the public.
